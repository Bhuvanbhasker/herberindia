About Academy

Our Vision

Herbourt college of india is a premier educational institution committed to fostering a love of learning and holistic growth in its students. Our mission is to provide a well-rounded education that shapes our students into emotionally balanced, physically fit, intellectually curious, and morally conscious individuals who can thrive in today's society.

Our Dedication

At Herbourt college of india, we believe in fostering a competitive atmosphere that challenges and inspires our students to reach their full potential. With state-of-the-art facilities and a range of extracurricular activities, including sports, debates, dramatics, and quizzes, our students are given the tools they need to succeed and shine.

School activities

(a) Games and Sports.

(b) Cultural Activities Dance, Music & Dramatics.

(c) Essay, Quiz Competitions, Debate.

(d) Chart and Scarapbbok Makeing

(e) Annual Functions and Annual Sport Day.

(f) Drawing,Painting,Craft.

(g) Variety of Co-Curricular activities are also conducted by the Senior students through the club of various faculties of Science, Commerce, Arts & Craft, media, sports, etc.


Student Facilities

(a) The school provides transport to the students, Buses carry the students to and from the school

(b) A well equipped library with books on various subjects for pre-primary & primary classes, picture reading & story-telling form a regular feature of instruction.

(c) The computer centre provides an adequate number of computers to impart computer education from class first onwards.

(d) Audio-visual equipments are provided to be used as teaching aids and also to screen educational & socially relevant programmers for the children.


Parent-Teachers Meet

(a) Parent-Teachers' meeting is held after every examination.

(b) Parents are requested to attend the meetings so that they can get an idea about their ward's progress in studies

(c) Parents are not allowed to see their children or meet their teachers during the school hours.

(d) Besides this, parents can meet their ward's class-teacher on every first Saturday of the month for the aforesaid purpose.


Syllabus List of Books and List of Holidays

Printed syllabus in the form of a booklet is provided by the school separately for every class. The course, divided for the three terminal examinations, is given in the syllabus

alongwith the names of the text books for each subject. List of Holidays, tentative school calendar of Co-Curricular activities and celebrations, including examination pattern for each class are also printed in this.


School Year

The school academic session begins from April and ends on the 15th May every year.

Admission Test

The admission test will cover English, Hindi, and Science based on the syllabus of the preceding class to which admission is sought.
